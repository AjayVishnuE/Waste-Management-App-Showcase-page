About

Our App features a new waste management system for rapidly growing cities. Instead of waiting for inefficient waste collection systems and unreliable pickup trucks to collect your waste, WRAP lets you request a pickup on-demand with just the tap of a button. WRAP platform is cheaper, efficient, and reliable than local incumbent systems, faster than their service, and Waste collection made easy and fantasized.

Features

Recycling101

We make sure that the waste has been delivered into the right hands and proper recycling has been conducted. The recycling reports are updated on WRAP instantly.

Traceability & Transparency

WRAP has some functionalities designed to bring transparency and traceability for the management and the relationship with its providers and customers.

WRAP Coins

WRAP is a web application that collects your recyclables and provides you rewards for them . Rewards will be in the form of wrap coins. Count of coins will be purely based on amount of waste.
